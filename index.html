Felipe A. Louza

About Me

I am currently Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Faculty of the Electrical Engineering of the Universidade Federal de Uberlândia, in Brazil.

In 2017, I received my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Campinas supervised by prof. Guilherme P. Telles and Simon Gog. My PhD thesis has been awarded as one of the best in the year by the Brazilian Computer Society and recieved the Honorable Mention in the CAPES award 2018.

From 2017 to 2019 I held a postdoc at the University of São Paulo and I have been Visiting Scientist at the BioAlgo group of the Consiglio Nazionale delle Ricerche, in Italy.

Research Interests

My research interest is the design of algorithms data structures for solving theoretical and applied problems in Bioinformatics, Information Retrieval, and related areas.
