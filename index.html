Felipe A. Louza

About Me

I am currently Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Faculty of the Electrical Engineering (FEELT) of the Universidade Federal de Uberlândia (UFU).

In 2017, I received my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Campinas supervised by prof. Guilherme P. Telles and Simon Gog.

From 2017 to 2019 I held a postdoc position at the University of São Paulo and I have been Visiting Scientist at the BioAlgo group of the Consiglio Nazionale delle Ricerche in Pisa.

Research Interests

My current research interest is the design of algorithms and data structures for solving problems in Bioinformatics, Information Retrieval, Data Compression, and related areas.
